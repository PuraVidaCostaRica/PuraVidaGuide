Work Schedule

In Costa Rica, the typical workweek is Monday to Friday, with weekends off (Saturday and Sunday). Most businesses operate from 8 AM to 5 PM, with a one-hour lunch break, but hours can vary depending on the sector and location.

Shops, offices, and public institutions close on public holidays, but many restaurants and tourist attractions remain open.

Public Holidays

Costa Rica celebrates several public holidays throughout the year. These holidays are official days off, and most businesses and schools are closed.

Date Holiday Description
January 1 New Year's Day Public holiday, a day for family and community gatherings.
May 1 Labor Day Public holiday, celebrated with parades and rallies.
August 2 Virgin of Los Ángeles Day A national holiday with a pilgrimage to Cartago.
September 15 Independence Day Public holiday with parades and cultural celebrations.
December 25 Christmas Day Public holiday celebrated with family feasts and gatherings.

Money Tips

  • US dollars are widely accepted in tourist areas, but you'll usually receive change in colones
  • Credit cards (Visa/Mastercard) are accepted in most establishments
  • ATMs ("cajeros automáticos") are widely available
  • Smaller denominations are helpful for taxis and small purchases
  • Always check exchange rates and count your change carefully
